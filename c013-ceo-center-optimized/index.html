About Company

CEO Center Phnom Penh

CEO Center is developed by Lixin Group in collaboration with KT Pacific Group, bringing together international experience in mixed-use real estate development.

The project integrates luxury residences, premium office space, hospitality, retail, and lifestyle amenities into one landmark destination in Phnom Penh.

Lixin Group is the primary developer behind CEO Center Phnom Penh, working with KT Pacific Group on a large mixed-use project that combines residences, Grade-A office spaces, and a five-star hotel operated by Wyndham.

CEO Center Phnom Penh, also referred to as Lixin CEO Center, is positioned as a prestigious mixed-use development in 7 Makara District, Phnom Penh.

  • Project name: CEO Center Phnom Penh (Lixin CEO Center)
  • Developer: Lixin Group with KT Pacific Group and Luxin Group JV
  • Location: 7 Makara District, Phnom Penh, Cambodia
  • Expected completion: 2026
  • Building height: 33 floors across 2 towers
  • Total units: 1,047 units
  • Project size: approximately 94,817 sqm

CEO Center is designed to become one of Phnom Penh's premier business and residential destinations by combining hospitality, modern offices, and luxury condominium living in one integrated development.

  • Prime CBD location close to major government institutions and business districts
  • Internationally managed Wyndham hotel apartments
  • Grade 6A smart office positioning
  • Luxury condominiums with premium lifestyle facilities
  • Long-term rental and capital growth potential
  • Foreign ownership pathway under Cambodia's Strata Title ownership framework
  • Wyndham-managed hotel apartments with a Guaranteed Rental Return (GRR) of up to 7% per year for the first 10 years. Subject to developer terms and conditions.
  • Premium Grade 6A office spaces designed for multinational corporations and institutional tenants.
  • Luxury residences with access to five-star hotel facilities.
  • Twin-tower architecture with more than 6,000 sqm of lifestyle amenities.

Foreign buyers can legally purchase eligible condominium units under Cambodia's Strata Title Law, allowing direct ownership of eligible residential units above the ground floor.

  • Freehold strata title ownership
  • Up to 70% foreign ownership quota
  • USD-denominated property market
  • Attractive investment environment for international buyers

Buyers should consult a qualified legal advisor before purchasing property in Cambodia.

Can foreigners buy units?
Yes. Eligible condominium units can be purchased by foreigners under Cambodia's Strata Title Law.

What types of units are available?
The project offers luxury condominiums, Wyndham-managed hotel apartments, and Grade 6A office units.

Is CEO Center a good investment?
CEO Center is positioned as a premium mixed-use development in Phnom Penh's CBD, suitable for owner-occupiers and long-term investors seeking rental income and capital appreciation.

When will the project be completed?
The estimated completion and handover are expected in 2026.

CEO Center is described as a 33-storey twin-tower mixed-use landmark situated along Czech Republic Blvd. (St. 169), Sangkat Veal Vong, Khan 7 Makara.

The development combines Grade A offices, luxury lifestyle apartments, and a five-star Wyndham Hotel under one roof, bringing business prestige together with modern urban living.

Facilities described in the client material include a 30-meter infinity pool, 275 sqm fitness center, 1,200 sqm sky bar, banquet hall, conference rooms, cigar lounge, spa, KTV rooms, 24/7 concierge services, CCTV surveillance, and card-access systems.
